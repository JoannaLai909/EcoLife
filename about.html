Origin

EcoLife Simulator was created to help players understand sustainability through everyday choices. Instead of only reading about environmental and social issues, users can experience how different decisions affect resources, communities, and long-term outcomes.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to connect the Sustainable Development Goals with interactive learning. Through game-based decision making, EcoLife Simulator encourages users to think about responsible consumption, environmental protection, social balance, and sustainable development in a more engaging way.

Visual Feedback

EcoLife Simulator provides visual feedback to help players understand the results of their decisions. Scores, progress bars, and ending images allow users to clearly see how their choices influence different sustainability goals.

Replayability

Different strategies lead to different endings, encouraging users to try again and explore new paths. By replaying the game, players can compare outcomes and learn how better decisions create more balanced and sustainable results.
